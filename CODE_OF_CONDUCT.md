# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in the
pi-privacy community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body
size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and
expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal
appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy toward other community members

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, insulting or derogatory comments, personal or
political attacks, publishing others' private information without permission, and other
conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the
maintainers via GitHub (open a private
[security advisory](https://github.com/privateer-agent/pi-privacy/security/advisories/new)
if you need a private channel). All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly
and fairly. Maintainers are obligated to respect the privacy and security of the reporter.
